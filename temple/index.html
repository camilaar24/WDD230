Site Name

Temple Inn & Suites

Site Purpose

Temple Inn and Suits creates and manages specialty hotel accommodations near temples throughout the world for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The overall purpose of this website is to advertise and provide information about Temple Inn & Suits' specialized services for temple guests who come to serve in the temple or who attend events like sealings, marriages, receptions, and youth trips.
