Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people between the age of 18 and over that are seeking to do a temple trip and enjoy of the ammenities and services offered to temple workers, patrons, and their families. These hotel accomodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by Temple Inn & Suits.
