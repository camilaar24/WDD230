Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people between the age of 18-80 that are seeking to start a business in which products are going to be eco-friendly or turn their current business more eco-friendly. This is for people who are enthusiastic about taking care of the planet and who are willing to volunteer occasionally to plant trees and keep Evergreen chamber clean from pollution and trash.

Persona One

Nathalia is 38 years old; she is a mom of 3 girls and at the same time, she is a full-time lawyer and makes around 90k a year. Even though she is always busy, she has been looking forward to having her own Beekeeping business since she is really concerned about bees, she knows that Beekeeping can also give her some income by having her own honey brand.

Persona Two

Cinthia is 22 years old and is about to get her bachelor's degree in digital animation. Cinthia is very passionate about taking care of the planet, she constantly asks her Instagram followers to recycle and make good use of water. Since she is very concerned about global warming, she started a small business of handmade bar soaps to reduce the use of plastics, she makes around 10k per year with her small business and is looking forward to making 30k this year through Evergreen Chamber.
